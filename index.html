Muhammed Ali's Life

  • 1942

    Muhammed Ali is born Casius Clay Junior in Louisville, Kentucky.

  • 1954

    Ali begins his boxing career as an Amateur boxer.

  • 1960

    Ali becoms an olympic champion and turn professional.

  • 1964

    Ali become Heavyweight chamopion and embraces the nation of Islam. He officially changes his name to Muhammed Ali.

  • 1967

    After a lot of thought and consideration Ali decides to refuse to fight in Vientam. He is accuess of "Draft-Dodging" but avoid prision term. Unforuntately he is stripped of his title as a result.

  • 1971

    The United State Supreme Court determine that they will reverse "Draft-Doging" decision.

  • 1974

    The famous "Rumble in the Jungle" resuslts in a win for Ali after he knocks out George Foreman

  • 1978

    Ali become the first man to ever win the Heavyweight titles 3 times.

  • 1980

    After a tough fight Ali is beaten by Larry Holmes. This same year he makes the decision to retire from boxing.

  • 1984

    Post boxing career effects take a toll on Ali's body. It is determind that he has Parkinson's Disease.

  • 1996

    Muhammed Ali has the great honor of lighting the Olympic cauldron at the opening of the 1996 Summer Atlanta Games.

  • 2012

    Despite a rough battle with Parkinson's, Ali make his final public appearance at the 2012 London Olympic Games.

  • June 3, 2016

    The People's Champion passes away at age 74 in Pheonix Arizona. An incredible man with an incredible career, he will not soon be forgotten.
